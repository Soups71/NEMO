NEMO is a research platform for exploring at maritime network security. It simulates sensors, sniffs traffic, and demonstrates just how easy you can mess with NMEA2000.

This platform focuses on lowering the bar for getting started with boat hacking. Typically to get started with boat hacking you would need thousands of dollars worth of equipment or a friend with a boat. Thankfully, with NEMO all you need is about 40 dollars.
